Key Questions

Start-upGrowthMaturity
(Cloud)(Blades)(Desktops)
Does a market exist? If so, in what form? How quickly can we adapt and scale? How can we sustain margin and share?

Start-up

Questions to gauge whether value exists at all:
  • Who is the customer?
  • How does our product create value for them?
  • Will anyone pay for what we offer? Can we make money on it?
  • Is this opportunity unique to us? What are the risks associated with it?

Growth

Questions to evaluate how well the design fits evolving customer needs:
  • What is customer response to early beta versions and prototypes?
  • What features should be prioritized in the next iteration?
  • What changes in technology should be incorporated in the next version?

Maturity

Questions to evaluate progress versus the plan:
  • How are we doing on schedule, features, and quality?
  • Does the design meet all of the exit criteria for this stage?
  • How much will it cost to complete the project?
